Rajat's list for the best Indian tea.

Ingredients

For the water:

  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Tea Powder

For the final:

  • Milk
  • Cardamon
  • Tulsi Leaves

Instructions

  1. Boil the water.
  2. After the water boils, add sugar and tea powder.
  3. Boil these for 2 minutes.
  4. Slowly add milk to these.
  5. Add Cardamon and tulsi leaves to it.
  6. Boil for 5 minutes.
  7. Your indian tea is ready.
